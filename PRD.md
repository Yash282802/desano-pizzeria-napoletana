# DeSano Pizzeria Napoletana Website Product Requirements Document
## Overview
DeSano Pizzeria Napoletana is a pizza restaurant business that aims to create a website to increase online presence, improve customer engagement, and drive sales. The website will provide an easy-to-use platform for customers to learn about the restaurant, view menus, make reservations, and order food online.

## Business Goals
The primary business goals of the DeSano Pizzeria Napoletana website are:
* Increase online visibility and reach a wider audience
* Improve customer engagement and loyalty
* Drive sales through online ordering and reservations
* Provide an easy-to-use platform for customers to interact with the restaurant
* Enhance the overall customer experience

## Target Audience
The target audience for the DeSano Pizzeria Napoletana website includes:
* Demographics: Foodies, families, and individuals aged 18-55
* Interests: Italian cuisine, pizza, dining out, and takeout
* Behaviors: People who regularly dine out or order takeout, and those who are interested in trying new restaurants
* Location: Primary target area is the local community, with a secondary target area of surrounding cities and towns

## Pages Needed
The following pages are required for the DeSano Pizzeria Napoletana website:
* **Home**: Introduction to the restaurant, featuring promotions, events, and a call-to-action to encourage visitors to explore the site further
* **Menu**: Detailed menu with descriptions, prices, and images of dishes
* **About**: Restaurant history, mission statement, and team profiles
* **Locations**: List of restaurant locations, including addresses, phone numbers, and hours of operation
* **Reservations**: Online reservation system for customers to book tables
* **Order Online**: Integration with a third-party food ordering platform or development of a custom ordering system
* **Events**: Calendar of upcoming events, including promotions, live music, and special deals
* **Contact**: Contact form, phone number, and email address for customer inquiries and feedback
* **Blog**: Optional page for publishing news, recipes, and behind-the-scenes stories

## Features
The DeSano Pizzeria Napoletana website will include the following features:
* **Responsive Design**: A mobile-friendly design that adapts to different screen sizes and devices
* **Online Ordering**: Integration with a third-party food ordering platform or development of a custom ordering system
* **Reservation System**: Online reservation system for customers to book tables
* **Menu Management**: Easy-to-use menu management system for administrators to update menu items and prices
* **Events Calendar**: Calendar of upcoming events, including promotions, live music, and special deals
* **Social Media Integration**: Links to social media profiles and integration with social media APIs for sharing content and promotions
* **Newsletter Signup**: Optional newsletter signup form for customers to receive exclusive promotions and updates
* **Search Engine Optimization (SEO)**: Optimization of website content and structure for improved search engine rankings

## Success Metrics
The success of the DeSano Pizzeria Napoletana website will be measured by the following metrics:
* **Website Traffic**: Number of unique visitors, page views, and bounce rate
* **Online Orders**: Number of online orders placed through the website
* **Reservations**: Number of reservations made through the website
* **Social Media Engagement**: Number of followers, likes, and shares on social media platforms
* **Customer Feedback**: Quality and quantity of customer feedback and reviews
* **Conversion Rate**: Percentage of visitors who complete a desired action (e.g., make a reservation, place an order)

## Technical Requirements
The DeSano Pizzeria Napoletana website will be built using the following technical requirements:
* **Content Management System (CMS)**: WordPress or a similar CMS
* **Programming Languages**: HTML, CSS, JavaScript, and PHP
* **Database**: MySQL or a similar database management system
* **Hosting**: Reliable web hosting with adequate storage and bandwidth
* **Security**: Implementation of security measures to protect customer data and prevent common web attacks

## Timeline
The development of the DeSano Pizzeria Napoletana website is expected to take approximately 12 weeks, with the following milestones:
* **Week 1-2**: Planning and research
* **Week 3-6**: Design and development
* **Week 7-10**: Testing and quality assurance
* **Week 11-12**: Launch and deployment

## Budget
The budget for the DeSano Pizzeria Napoletana website is estimated to be $15,000, broken down into:
* **Design and development**: $10,000
* **Content creation**: $2,000
* **Testing and quality assurance**: $1,000
* **Launch and deployment**: $1,000
* **Miscellaneous (project management, etc.)**: $1,000

Note: The budget and timeline are estimates and may vary depending on the complexity of the project and the requirements of the client.
